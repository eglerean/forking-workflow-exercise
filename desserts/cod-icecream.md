# Cod Ice Cream Sundae

## Ingredients:
- 1 cup cooked and flaked cod
- 2 cups vanilla ice cream
- 1/2 cup caramel sauce
- 1/4 cup crushed chocolate cookies
- Whipped cream for topping
- Maraschino cherry for garnish

## Instructions:
1. Start by preparing the cod. Ensure it is cooked thoroughly and flake it into small pieces.
2. Take a serving glass or bowl and layer the bottom with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
3. Sprinkle a handful of the cooked cod flakes evenly over the ice cream.
4. Drizzle a generous amount of caramel sauce on top of the cod layer.
5. Add another scoop of vanilla ice cream, followed by another layer of flaked cod.
6. Drizzle more caramel sauce over the cod layer and sprinkle crushed chocolate cookies on top.
7. Finish off with a dollop of whipped cream and garnish with a maraschino cherry.
8. Serve immediately and enjoy the surprising blend of flavors.
